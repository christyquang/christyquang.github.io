DESIGN PROJECTS

After starting college, I wanted to discover whatever field would motivate me to further study since I’ve always heard about people finding what they’re passionate about in college. CS and DS are interesting but I don’t have the excitement to constantly learn more within those fields. Growing up, I was frequently teased for not being artistic or having creativity since my handwriting is messy and I couldn't draw decently enough like other females, causing me to avoid anything art-related. I took some art/design courses in college because I was never involved in those avenues previously and wanted to experiment (plus fulfill my general education requirements).

After taking DESINV 22 (Prototyping and Fabrication) @ UC Berkeley, I discovered that I enjoy design (physical and UI/UX) and while I’m not the conventional designer who specializes in making things look aesthetically pleasing, I lean towards physical design and creating useful products since I prioritize optimization. I've completed Berkeley's Certificate in Design Innovation and SCET's Certificate in Entrepreneurship & Technology due to my interest in design and hope to get more involved with human-centered interaction research and robotics. In the meantime, my goal is to one day own my personal 3D printer and laser cutter (Glowforge 👀) in my home.
